Bacteria Around Us

Have you ever wondered about the invisible world of bacteria surrounding us? From the surfaces we touch daily to the air we breathe, bacteria are everywhere. These microscopic organisms play essential roles in ecosystems and human health, but they can also cause contamination and disease.

In this experiment, we will explore the fascinating world of bacteria. You'll learn how to:

  • Collect bacteria samples from various surfaces.
  • Grow bacterial colonies using agar in Petri dishes.
  • Observe and analyse the size, shape, and colour of bacterial colonies.

By conducting this activity, you'll gain insight into the diversity of bacteria in our environment and the importance of hygiene in minimizing harmful microbial exposure. Are you ready to uncover the unseen microbial world? Let's begin!
